Fried Rice Recipe

This recipe is one of the easiest dishes to make, if you want a quick to cook meal.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Garlic.(Amount depends on the size portion.)
  • Egg.(Amount depends on the size portion.)
  • Soy Sauce.
  • Salt and Pepper to taste.

Guide to cook

  1. Heat up the pan with oil.
  2. Add in first the garlic.
  3. Cook garlic untill some of them are fried, if cooked too much, other pieces of garlic will be burned.
  4. Add the eggs and scramble them.
  5. Once the eggs are cooked, add in the day old rice.
  6. Mix the rice, while mixing break big pieces to rice to loosen them, it is best to break all rice chunk.
  7. Once finish, add in the soy sauce and mix.
  8. When you are finish mixing them, add salt and pepper, add just enough so the seasonings won't overpower the other ingredients
  9. When the taste is to your liking, transfer the rice to a bowl, and enjoy your egg fried rice.
